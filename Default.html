about this game

Angel Guardian is a captivating side-scrolling action shooter where players embody Kita, a young warrior guardian on a besieged Space Station kingdom of Alterra . Charged with safeguarding a prince from subversive cybermagic wielding abilities akin to magic, players employ action shooting, stealth, and hacking to triumph over foes and escape as the entire station becomes your foe.

Engage in adrenaline-pumping battles requiring both strategy and quick reflexes. Choose from an array of high-tech or present-day weapons and double- upgrade them to unleash devastating firepower. Alternatively, wield a trusty melee sword for up-close combat, slashing through enemies with precision.

Harness the power of hacking not just to manipulate enemies into aiding your cause but also to bypass security systems, crack codes, and gain access to hidden areas vital to your mission. Utilize stealth tactics to slip past vigilant cybermage patrols and outmaneuver formidable obstacles undetected.

Immerse yourself in versatile gameplay roles: become a relentless weapons specialist, a swift and lethal melee combatant, a shadowy ghost navigating unseen, or a skilled hacker unraveling digital puzzles.

Fulfill your destiny as the ultimate guardian by guiding the prince to safety. Mentor as you protect him to unlock his latent potential as a powerful warrior and digital wizard that can be a true heir to the kingdom.

Explore a richly detailed space station teeming with secrets, uncover the true motives of the cybermages, and restore peace to the kingdom in this unique and immersive gaming experience.

  • Collect 8 double-upgradeable rifles plus a sword.
  • Use the environmental hazards as weapons including gas and fire barrels.
  • Use stealth to sneak past enemies undetected.
  • Help the prince maneuver through intense platforming action.
  • Mentor the prince to unlock his cyber-magic abilities.
  • Work together with the prince to learn simultaneous acrobatic combos and powerful attack moves.
  • Master hacking to get past doors and other digital locks before alarms bring enemies your way.
  • Battle diverse enemies and devastating bosses.
  • Gather high-tech items like invisibility cloaks, shields or jet packs to help you escape
